Cestrum nocturnum, the lady of the night, night-blooming jasmine, night-blooming jessamine, night-scented jessamine, night-scented cestrum or poisonberry,[3] is a species of plant in the potato family Solanaceae. It is native to Mexico, Central America and northern South America[1] and is naturalized in South Asia.[4]

Despite its common name, the species is not a "true jasmine" and not of the genus Jasminum.
